IIITK Sarthi 🚀
The Smart Campus Operating System for IIIT Kalyani

IIITK Sarthi is a next-generation AI-powered campus companion platform designed for the students, faculty, staff, and administration of IIIT Kalyani.

Unlike traditional college websites or ERP systems, IIITK Sarthi aims to become a complete digital campus ecosystem — bringing academics, productivity, communication, campus services, opportunities, and AI assistance into one modern platform.

🌐 Vision

“Everything a student needs — in 30 seconds.”

IIITK Sarthi is designed to eliminate:

scattered WhatsApp groups
buried PDF notices
outdated portals
repetitive manual searching
disconnected campus tools

and replace them with a unified, modern, AI-powered campus experience.
